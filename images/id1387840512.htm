    Description

    Order your favorite night meal

    Currently serving in Bangalore, Mangalore, Hubli, Manipal, Hyderabad, Mysore and Derlakatte

    Knight Bite is a food delivery app which will help anyone to get their favorite meal without going out of their current place. You can order any kind of meals such as burgers, shakes, biryani, coolers, and desserts and you will get it within 35 minutes. Not only that! Our app contains a very rich menu that will give you the possibility to choose any kind of food you need and order it from 7pm to 4am.

    Knight Bite is perfect for people who like to get night meals without going out of their bed or house. And the best thing is that you can start ordering from the app and pay online or choose the cash on delivery option.

    Why do you need to download and install Knight Bite on your smartphone or tablet instead of other food delivery apps?

    - A very easy to use interface that will let you place the order easily. The concept of getting a meal should be easier for everyone and without many steps.
    - Very rich and complete menu to ensure that you get the meal that you like.
    - Place an order anytime from 7pm to 4am.
    - The delivery time takes about 35 minutes.
    - Real-time order status to track where your meal is.
    - We offer a very wide selection of payment methods. You can pay using Credit/debit card, Paytm, UPI or Net banking. You can also choose to pay once you get the meal.

    What are you waiting for? Download Knight Bite App and enjoy ordering your favorite meals online.
